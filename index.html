Healthy Lifestyle

Nutrition

Eating a balanced diet helps maintain energy and supports overall health. Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your meals.

Exercise

Exercise helps improve physical fitness, mental well-being, and overall health. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.

Sleep

Good sleep is essential for health. Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support your body's recovery and well-being.
